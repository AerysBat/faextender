Features

FurAffinity Extender has the following features.

  • Adds a download button to image pages
  • Adds an 'open folder' link to image pages to open the folder it's saved to
  • Adds an 'open folder' link to user pages
  • Adds an open gallery or submissions page in tabs link to open up all images in a gallery or submission page at once (with optional delay to avoid timeouts)
  • Tested regularly on Windows, Linux, and Mac OS X
  • Open source

Hidden options

There are a few hidden options that can be changed from about:config.

  • extensions.faext.openintabs.delaytime - Adjusts the delay for Open in Tabs, in seconds
  • extensions.faext.openintabs.reverse - Reverses tab order (set to false for pre-2.1 behaviour)
